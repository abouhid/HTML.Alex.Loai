Freddie Mercury

The Rock and Roll Legend

Here's a timeline of Freddie Mercury's life:

  • 1946 - Born Farrokh Bulsara; 5 September in Zanzibar
  • 1970 - Formed Queen in 1970 with guitarist Brian May and drummer Roger Taylor.
  • 1985 - Live Aid concert, considered the best live performance of the band.
  • 1991 - Mercury died in 1991 at age 45.

“I won't be a rock star. I will be a legend.”

-- Mercury, Freddie
